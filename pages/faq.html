Frequently Asked Questions

How do I begin planning my trip with Around the Globe?

Your journey begins with a single step — a conversation. Simply visit our Booking page and complete the brief consultation form. A member of our travel concierge team will reach out to you personally to curate an itinerary tailored to your pace, preferences, and dreams.

Do you offer fully customized itineraries?

Absolutely. Every experience we craft is bespoke. From private yacht charters and heritage estate stays to last-minute Michelin reservations — we believe your journey should reflect you, and nothing less.

Can I request a specific destination or experience?

Of course. Whether it’s dining beneath the northern lights in Lapland or learning pasta-making in a 17th-century Tuscan villa, we specialize in turning personal dreams into beautifully orchestrated realities.

Are your services available internationally?

Yes. Our curated experiences span the globe — from the snow-covered chalets of the Swiss Alps to the serene rice fields of Ubud. Wherever you wish to wander, we have the connections to elevate your experience.

What type of accommodations do you provide?

Only the finest. From boutique countryside manors to presidential suites in legendary five-star hotels, every stay is personally vetted for elegance, discretion, and distinction.

Do you offer culinary travel experiences?

Indeed. Our Culinary Trips include vineyard tastings, private chef masterclasses, and seatings at some of the world’s most celebrated tables. We believe that taste is a destination of its own.

What if I require privacy or security while traveling?

Discretion is our signature. Whether you’re a public figure, a high-profile client, or simply value privacy, we work with trusted partners to ensure every detail is handled with the utmost confidentiality and care.

Is Around the Globe suitable for solo travelers or couples?

Absolutely. Whether you seek soulful solitude or romantic seclusion, our team will tailor the experience to fit your vision — from private retreats to hidden getaways designed for two.

How far in advance should I book?

For the most exclusive experiences and accommodations, we recommend planning at least 3 to 6 months in advance. However, we are no strangers to crafting exceptional last-minute escapes with grace and precision.

Is there a membership or loyalty program?

Yes. Our Exclusive Travel Club offers returning clients access to private perks, priority bookings, and first invitations to unreleased experiences. Details are shared upon your second curated journey with us.

Still Have Questions?

Our concierge team is always here to assist. For bespoke inquiries or anything not listed above, please contact us directly at hello@aroundtheglobe.com or via the Booking page.

Around the Globe is more than a travel brand — it’s a passport to the extraordinary.
