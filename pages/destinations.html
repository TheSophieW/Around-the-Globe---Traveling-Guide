Destinations – Explore the World, Your Way

Where Luxury Meets Discovery

Travel isn’t just about going places—it’s about experiencing them in a way that feels personal, effortless, and unforgettable. At Around the Globe, we’ve handpicked the most extraordinary destinations—from timeless cities to secluded escapes—so you can explore the world with exclusive access and curated experiences.

Our Signature Destinations

We don’t just list places. We craft journeys. Whether you crave beachfront serenity, cosmopolitan indulgence, or off-the-grid luxury, we have the perfect escape waiting for you.

    Island Retreats – Where Time Stands Still

  • Maldives – Overwater bungalows, private island resorts, and crystal-clear waters as far as the eye can see.
  • Bora Bora, French Polynesia – Ultimate barefoot luxury, exclusive spa retreats, and sunset dining over the lagoon.
  • Seychelles – Untouched nature, hidden beaches, and eco-luxury at its finest.

    Cosmopolitan Escapes – The World’s Most Elegant Cities

  • Paris, France – A timeless masterpiece—Michelin-starred dining, haute couture shopping, and private art tours.
  • Dubai, UAE – Where innovation meets indulgence—private desert safaris, sky-high luxury, and gold-infused spa treatments.
  • Tokyo, Japan – The perfect fusion of tradition and futurism—fine dining, ancient temples, and members-only sake tastings.

    Nature & Adventure – Beyond the Beaten Path

  • Swiss Alps, Switzerland – Chalet luxury, VIP ski resorts, and après-ski champagne lounges.
  • Patagonia, Chile – Ultimate solitude—glacial hikes, luxury eco-lodges, and helicopter tours over untouched landscapes.
  • Great Barrier Reef, Australia – Private yacht diving experiences, underwater dining, and hidden coral cays.

    Cultural & Culinary Journeys – Taste the World’s Best

  • Tuscany, Italy – Vineyard hopping, private pasta-making classes, and truffle hunting with local experts.
  • Kyoto, Japan – Authentic Kaiseki dining, ancient tea ceremonies, and traditional Ryokan stays.
  • Marrakech, Morocco – Secret rooftop dinners, spice market tours, and exclusive riad accommodations.

Beyond Destinations – Experience the World Like an Insider

What makes a place unforgettable isn’t just where you go, but how you experience it. That’s why we go beyond the expected itineraries and offer:

  • Private yacht charters to explore secluded islands.
  • Elite guided city tours with local connoisseurs.
  • Luxury train journeys through the most breathtaking landscapes
