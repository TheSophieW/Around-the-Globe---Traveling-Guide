Culinary Journeys: A Taste of Luxury Around the World

Savor the Finest Flavors, Crafted for the Elite Traveler

Indulge in a world where gastronomy meets grandeur. At Around the Globe, we curate extraordinary culinary experiences that take you beyond the plate—blending luxury, exclusivity, and adventure into every bite. Whether you dream of Michelin-starred dining, a private yacht tasting experience, or hands-on cooking masterclasses with world-renowned chefs, we ensure every moment is as indulgent as it is unforgettable.

Dine at Sea: The Ultimate Cruise Gastronomy Experience

Imagine an evening aboard a private luxury cruise, sailing into the sunset while savoring a six-course gourmet meal designed by a world-class chef. From freshly caught Mediterranean seafood to handcrafted desserts served under the stars, every dish is a celebration of flavor and elegance.

    Highlights:

  • Exclusive Chef’s Table with Michelin-starred menus
  • Sommelier-curated wine pairings featuring rare vintages
  • Personalized, ocean-view dining for a truly intimate experience

Michelin-Starred Indulgence: A Seat at the World’s Best Tables

For those who seek nothing but the finest, we grant you VIP access to the most coveted Michelin-starred restaurants across the globe. Dine at the hands of culinary legends, experiencing artistry in every bite—from the delicate truffle-infused masterpieces of Paris to the avant-garde tasting menus of Tokyo.

    Featured Destinations:

  • Le Grand Luxe, Paris – A three-star Michelin wonder set in a historical château, offering gastronomic excellence with an unparalleled wine collection.
  • Kyoto’s Kaiseki Experience – A deep dive into Japan’s refined multi-course tradition, served in a private tea house.
  • New York’s Skyline Dining – Enjoy modern haute cuisine with panoramic city views, where every dish is a masterpiece.

Beyond the Plate: Exclusive Tastings & Wine Retreats

Travel through rolling vineyards and hidden cellars, where private sommeliers unlock the secrets of the world’s most exquisite wines and spirits. Experience the art of Champagne sabrage in the heart of France, sip on centuries-old Bordeaux, or indulge in handcrafted whiskey tastings in Scotland’s private estates.

    Exclusive Experiences Include:

  • Helicopter tours over Tuscany’s famed vineyards, landing for a private wine tasting
  • VIP access to Bordeaux’s finest cellars, with guided tastings led by award-winning vintners
  • Cognac & whiskey blending masterclasses, where you create your own signature spirit

Culinary Masterclasses: Cook with the World's Best

Take your passion for food to the next level with immersive culinary workshops led by the world’s top chefs. From the art of pasta-making in Italy to perfecting sushi in Japan, these hands-on sessions let you craft and taste authentic cuisine at its finest.

    Handpicked Workshops:

  • Italian Pasta Perfection – Knead, roll, and shape fresh handmade pasta in a private villa in Tuscany, paired with local wines.
  • Sushi & Sake in Tokyo – A private lesson with a master sushi chef, followed by a sake-pairing experience.
  • Parisian Pastry Secrets – Learn to craft flawless croissants, macarons, and éclairs in an exclusive patisserie workshop.
