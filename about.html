About Us – Around the Globe

Who We Are

At Around the Globe, we don’t just explore; we curate experiences. What started as a passion for travel turned into a mission—to bring the world’s most exclusive destinations, hidden gems, and elite experiences to travelers who crave more than just a vacation.
We're the team behind the journey, blending a love for adventure, a deep appreciation for culture, and a commitment to luxury travel. Whether it’s a private sunset yacht cruise in Santorini, a Michelin-starred culinary tour through Paris, or an off-grid, VIP safari in Africa, we believe travel should be seamless, immersive, and unforgettable.

Meet the Creators

We’re more than just a travel guide. We’re explorers, storytellers, and experience architects. Each one of us brings a unique touch to Around the Globe:

Guy Mor

Experience Architect & Logistics Virtuoso

With over a decade in the world of elite hospitality and private event curation, Guy is the calm behind the scenes — the one who ensures every moment unfolds seamlessly. From coordinating private jet itineraries across five time zones to orchestrating candlelit dinners on remote Aegean cliffs, he’s mastered the art of precision wrapped in elegance. Guy’s background spans luxury concierge services, boutique hotel consulting, and executive-level planning for high-profile clients. But what sets him apart is his philosophy: “Luxury isn’t about excess — it’s about intention.” At Around the Globe, Guy designs journeys where timing, ambiance, and experience align to create the unforgettable — every time.

Sophie Wissotskoi

With a keen eye for detail and a deep love for storytelling, Sophie founded Around the Globe as a way to bring meaningful, elevated travel to those who seek more than just destinations — but moments of beauty, intention, and wonder.
